Energy and forces computation in simulations.

In order to compute an energy or a force, two things are needed: a function mapping distance/angles to energy/force, and a way to compute theses functions. The functions implements the PotentialFunction trait, and the PotentialComputation trait provides ways to effectively compute the energy: with a cutoff, from a table, etc.
